WTS: \\
Given that $\Gamma \mapsto \Omega$, $A=[\Gamma]A$, $A\not\in \text{FV}(A)$\\
if $[\Omega]\Gamma\vdash [\Omega]\alphahat \leq [\Omega]A$, then there are some $\Delta,\Omega'$ such that $\Delta \mapsto \Omega'$, $\Omega \longrightarrow \Omega'$ and $\Gamma \vdash \alphahat \instl A \dashv \Delta$.\\
if $[\Omega]\Gamma\vdash [\Omega]A \leq [\Omega]\alphahat$, then there are some $\Delta,\Omega'$ such that $\Delta \mapsto \Omega'$, $\Omega \longrightarrow \Omega'$ and $\Gamma \vdash A \instr \alphahat \dashv \Delta$.
